Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world and is known for his aggressive playing style and consistency in all formats of the game.

Kohli has numerous records to his name, including being the fastest player to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs). He has also led India to many memorable victories, including a historic Test series win in Australia in 2018-19.

Off the field, Virat Kohli is known for his dedication to fitness, his entrepreneurial ventures, and his philanthropic efforts. He is an inspiration to millions and continues to break records and set new benchmarks in cricket.
